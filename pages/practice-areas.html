Corporate & Commercial

From company formation to transformative M&A—comprehensive legal support for every stage of your business journey.

Our Corporate & Commercial practice forms the backbone of Crimson & Quill's service offering, providing clients with comprehensive legal support across the entire lifecycle of their business operations. We advise domestic enterprises, multinational corporations, financial institutions, private equity funds, and entrepreneurs on the full range of corporate matters.

Corporate & M&A

Company formations, reorganisations, governance, and complex M&A transactions.

Private Equity & Venture Capital

Equity investments, mezzanine financing, term sheets, and shareholder arrangements.

Joint Ventures & Strategic Alliances

Formation, governance, and dissolution of collaborative business arrangements.

Corporate Migration

Re-domiciliation of corporate entities into and out of Uganda.

02

Banking, Finance & Real Property

Expert guidance at the intersection of capital, commerce, and tangible assets.

Our Banking, Finance & Real Property practice advises lenders, borrowers, investors, and developers on the full spectrum of financing transactions, from bilateral lending arrangements and syndicated facilities through to complex project financings and structured credit products.

Debt Finance

Term loans, revolving facilities, syndicated lending, and bridge financing.

Project Development & Finance

Infrastructure financings across energy, transportation, and social infrastructure.

Mortgages & Secured Lending

Security creation, perfection, and enforcement over real property.

Financial Restructuring & Insolvency

Workouts, administration, receivership, and liquidation proceedings.

Regulatory, Compliance & Risk

Navigate complex regulatory frameworks with confidence and transform compliance into competitive advantage.

In an era of expanding regulatory oversight and heightened stakeholder expectations, businesses must navigate an increasingly complex web of legal obligations. Our practice provides the guidance necessary to operate confidently within this environment.

Advisory & Regulatory

Licensing, regulatory compliance, and engagement with sector regulators.

Data, Privacy & Cybersecurity

Data protection compliance, privacy programmes, and incident response.

Tax

Tax planning, compliance, disputes, and cross-border structuring.

Sustainability & Responsible Business

Environmental compliance, ESG, and climate-related legal matters.

Industry & Infrastructure

Powering Uganda's transformation—legal expertise for major projects in construction, mining, technology, and energy.

Construction

FIDIC contracts, EPC agreements, and construction dispute resolution.

Mining & Natural Resources

Mining licences, development agreements, and resource transactions.

Technology Transactions

Software licensing, SaaS agreements, and technology procurement.

Energy & Project Finance

Power projects, PPAs, and energy regulatory matters.

Employment, Compensation & Benefits

Dispute Resolution

When disputes arise, we deploy the full spectrum of resolution strategies—from negotiation to international arbitration.

International Arbitration
